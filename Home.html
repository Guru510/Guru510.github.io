Welcome to NBKR College

About Us

NBKR Engineering College is a leading engineering institution located in the heart of the city. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in engineering and technology, designed to prepare students for successful careers in the industry.

Our faculty members are experienced professionals with a passion for teaching and research. They are committed to providing students with a high-quality education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills.

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and recreational areas. We also offer a variety of extracurricular activities, such as sports, cultural events, and student clubs, to help students develop their skills and interests outside the classroom.

Programs

  • Computer Science and Engineering

    Our Computer Science and Engineering program provides students with a solid foundation in computer science theory and practice. Students will learn programming, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and computer systems.

  • Electrical and Communication Engineering

    Our Electrical and Communication Engineering program covers the design, implementation, and management of electrical and communication systems. Students will learn about power systems, control systems, communication networks, and signal processing.

  • Electrical Engineering

    Our Electrical Engineering program focuses on the design, implementation, and maintenance of electrical systems. Students will learn about power systems, control systems, electronics, and electrical machines.

  • Mechanical Engineering

    Our Mechanical Engineering program covers the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems. Students will learn about thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, and mechanical design.

  • Civil Engineering

    Our Civil Engineering program focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of civil infrastructure. Students will learn about structural analysis, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, and environmental engineering.
